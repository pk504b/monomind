# Monomind: Your Second Brain in Your New Tab

Transform your New Tab into a minimal, high-performance workspace designed to capture intent and archive your journey.

---

Most "New Tab" extensions distract you with news feeds and clutter. Monomind does the opposite. It provides a digital altar - a minimalist, private space designed to capture your intent and protect your focus. Most productivity tools fragment your focus across browser tabs, apps, and windows. Monomind brings the essentials together right where you spend most of your time.

## 🧠 Your Second Brain, Organized by Time

Monomind treats time as the primary filing system for your thoughts. It combines a distraction-free writing space, a dual-layer task management, and an integrated Pomodoro timer — all synced to a powerful time-travel interface. It's a private, local-first tool that respects your privacy and helps you stay focused on what matters.

## ✨ Features

### 🖋️ Daily Notes

- **Deep Writing**: A distraction-free, rich-text editor to document reflections, brain-dumps, or daily intentions. Supports Markdown.
- **Date-Indexed Notes**: Every day starts with a blank slate. Switch between dates to view your archival history.
- **Auto-Save**: Never lose a thought with real-time IndexedDB persistence.

### ✅ Dual-Layer Task Management

- **Today's Focus**: Todos for the day - list and conquer. Satisfaction and achievement at the end of the day.
- **Later Queue**: A global, persistent list for big-picture ideas and future commitments.

### ⏱️ Integrated Pomodoro

- **Deep Work Sessions**: Built-in 25/5/15 minute timers to help you get into your flow state.
- **Session Logging**: Automatically tracks and logs completed sessions to your daily history.

### 📅 Time Travel & Anchoring

- **Calendar Integration**: A seamless calendar to navigate back to see past reflections or forward to plan future victories.
- **The "Today" Anchor**: Instantly return to the present with a single click.

### 🌓 Aesthetic and Private

- **Modern Minimalist UI**: Clean, modern UI system and handpicked typography. No bloat, no distractions.
- **Smart Themes**: Support for Light, Dark, and System modes, perfectly synchronized with your OS.
- **Privacy by Design**: Your data is stored locally. No servers, no tracking, no accounts. Just you and your thoughts.

## 🛠️ Technical Stack

- **Framework**: [React](https://reactjs.org/) + [Vite](https://vitejs.dev/)
- **Styling**: [Tailwind CSS](https://tailwindcss.com/) + [shadcn/ui](https://ui.shadcn.com/)
- **Database**: [Dexie.js](https://dexie.org/) (High-performance IndexedDB wrapper)
- **Rich Text**: [Tiptap](https://tiptap.dev/)
- **Icons**: [Lucide React](https://lucide.dev/)
- **Date Handling**: [date-fns](https://date-fns.org/)
